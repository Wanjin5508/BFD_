Assignment

Now we’d like you to think about one of these situations which we have described above. Even if these routine tasks appear simple to you, try to imagine how  one of the users from our personas list would do the same. For example consider how Carole who is blind, would buy a dress or a phone online, or how Tom who has speech impairment would communicate with his sister who is living in another continent? Now try to imagine as precisely as possible how they can do the task under consideration  and what they would need to complete it.
